About Us

Imaginatively conceived and deftly executed, this Modern Masterpiece incorporates glass, metal and marble to create beautiful lines of vision. Entering this home will energize you -- natural light is everywhere and the expanse of white walls and floors allows the light to constantly change the character of each room. The brilliant kitchen pops with color and functionality.

Outdoors, the grounds feature waterfalls, a glass tiled pool, spa, grotto, fire pits and outdoor cabanas all resembling the finest resort living. The home also features a ten seat media room and bowling alley -- all behind the private gates of Bell Canyon.

Established in 2002 by Omar Nassery ON development corp has built beautiful contemporary homes in the Los Angeles area. On area that has been their favorite is Bell Canyon, CA where they have built 4 homes and are in the process of building 2 more.

Along with building homes they have also been involve in building the Sunset Marquis hotel. Their style is contemporary-modern with extensive use of natural light. Open floor plans with an emphasis on indoor and outdoor connection. Large backyards with resort style living and built for entertaining.
