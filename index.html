Featured on HDTV's Design Star

Imaginatively conceived and deftly executed, this Modern Masterpiece incorporates glass, metal and marble to create beautiful lines of vision. Entering this home will energize you -- natural light is everywhere and the expanse of white walls and floors allows the light to constantly change the character of each room. The brilliant kitchen pops with color and functionality. Outdoors, the grounds feature waterfalls, a glass tiled pool, spa, grotto, fire pits and outdoor cabanas all resembling the finest resort living. The home also features a ten seat media room and bowling alley -- all behind the private gates of Bell Canyon.
